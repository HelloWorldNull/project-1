Zis is a paragraph about TF2's Ze Spy.

    Backstabbing
  1. Use your cloak to get behind the enemy, but uncloak behind walls or structures to muffle the uncloak sound.
  2. Uncloak while disguised without being seen so if anyone sees you while you are on your way to get a backstab, no one calls you out.
  3. Do. Not. Uncloak. Near an enemy or Sentry while undisguised, they will shoot you instantly.
    Review
  • Cloak helps to get behind the enemy, muffle the noise.
  • Keep a disguise on while cloaked so you always appear as an enemy.
  • Be careful of where you uncloak and be aware if you have a disguise on or not.
